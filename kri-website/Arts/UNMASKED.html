UNMASKED The task of titling this item seemed overwhelming to me. Giving your creation a title is a difficult step of the process. The caption is an opportunity to add words to the image and nudge it in a specific direction. You want to do the work fair without restricting its possibilities by locking it in. But the more I sought to give it wings, the more I discovered I was just exposing my personal boundaries in relation to the art. Something great happens when you let go of a work and share it. It becomes a minor puzzle piece in the artistic process of others. Thank you for taking part in this process and giving your opinions on the work.
